## Interview 1: Technical Co-founder
- **Name**: Sarah J.
- **Role**: CTO & Co-founder
- **Company Stage**: Seed (12 employees)
- **Direct Quote**: "I know we're overspending, but I don't have time to log into five different portals every month to check who's still using what."
- **Key Pain Point**: Redundancy. Half the team uses Cursor, the other half uses Copilot, but everyone still has a ChatGPT Plus account "just in case."
- **What Surprised Me**: She was more concerned about the *time* it takes to manage seats than the actual dollar amount.
- **Design Change**: Added the "Tool Consolidation" rule specifically because of this interview.

## Interview 2: Engineering Lead
- **Name**: David K.
- **Role**: Staff Engineer / Team Lead
- **Company Stage**: Series A (45 employees)
- **Direct Quote**: "The annual billing discount seems small for one person, but when I saw it was $1,500/year for the whole team, it became a no-brainer."
- **Key Pain Point**: Communication gap. He wants his team to have the best tools, but he doesn't want to look irresponsible to the CFO.
- **What Surprised Me**: He wanted a "shareable link" specifically so he could send it to the finance department without having to explain the math.
- **Design Change**: Prioritized the "Shareable Report" feature and the "Savings Hero" visualization.

## Interview 3: Freelance Developer / Solo Founder
- **Name**: Alex M.
- **Role**: Senior Dev / Indie Hacker
- **Company Stage**: Pre-seed / Bootstrapped
- **Direct Quote**: "For me, every $20/mo matters. I'm paying for GPT-4o twice through different APIs and I didn't even realize it."
- **Key Pain Point**: Lack of visibility into API usage vs subscription overlap.
- **What Surprised Me**: He didn't want to create an account. "If I have to sign up for yet another thing just to see if I can save money, I'm out."
- **Design Change**: Reinforced the "No Login Required" philosophy and moved the email capture to the *end* of the value chain.
