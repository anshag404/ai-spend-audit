# Unit Economics & Funnel Math

## The Funnel (Assumptions)
- **Website Visits**: 10,000 / month
- **Audit Completion Rate**: 15% (1,500 audits/mo)
- **Lead Capture Rate**: 20% of audits (300 leads/mo)
- **High-Savings Leads (> $500/yr)**: 10% of total leads (30 leads/mo)

## Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)
- **Organic Content (Twitter/Reddit)**: $0 (Time intensive)
- **Paid Search (Targeting "SaaS spend management")**: $15.00 per completed audit.
- **Estimated Blended CAC**: $4.50 per audit completion.

## Converted Lead Value (CLV)
We define a "Converted Lead" as someone who signs up for the premium monitoring service ($49/mo bundle).
- **Conversion from Lead to Paid**: 5%
- **Average Revenue Per User (ARPU)**: $49/mo
- **Churn Rate**: 8% (Monthly)
- **Estimated Lifetime Value (LTV)**: ($49 / 0.08) = $612.50

## The Path to $1M ARR
To reach $1,000,000 in Annual Recurring Revenue, we need ~$83,333 in MRR.
- **Required Customers**: 1,700 active paid users at $49/mo.
- **Required Funnel Scale**: 
  - To get 1,700 customers at a 5% lead-to-paid conversion, we need 34,000 leads.
  - At a 20% audit-to-lead rate, we need 170,000 completed audits.
  - At a 15% visit-to-audit rate, we need **1.13 Million annual visitors**.

## Profitability
- **Gross Margin**: 92% (Low infra costs, high automation).
- **Break-even point**: 150 paid users covers all current operational and developmental costs.
